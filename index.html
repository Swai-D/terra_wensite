Business Formation & Registration

End-to-end company registration through BRELA for Tanzanian entrepreneurs and foreign investors alike.

What's Included

  • Company registration via BRELA (Business Registration & Licensing Agency)
  • Business name search, reservation, and approval
  • Memorandum & Articles of Association preparation
  • TRA Tax Identification Number (TIN) registration
  • Business License application (local authority & sector-specific)
  • TIC (Tanzania Investment Centre) registration for foreign investors
  • EPZ (Export Processing Zone) registration
  • Sole Proprietorship and Partnership registration

Available Business Structures

  • Private Limited Company (Ltd)
  • Public Limited Company (PLC)
  • Branch Office of a Foreign Company
  • Representative / Liaison Office
  • Sole Proprietorship / Business Name

Key Steps We Handle For You

  • Choose the right legal structure for your business
  • Register your business name with BRELA
  • Prepare and submit all required documents
  • Register with TRA and obtain your business license

We handle all government submissions, paperwork, and follow-ups on your behalf — saving you time and eliminating costly errors. Foreign investors are especially welcome; we understand the specific legal requirements that apply to you.

Immigration & Work Permits

Full immigration support for foreign nationals working or investing in Tanzania.

Work Permit Services

  • Class A Work Permit — for investors and self-employed foreigners
  • Class B Work Permit — for employees sponsored by a Tanzanian company
  • Work permit renewals and extensions
  • Special Pass applications (short-term)

Visa & Residence Permits

  • Business Visa processing and applications
  • Residence Permit Class A (investor), B (employee), C (dependent)
  • Dependent Pass for family members of permit holders
  • Immigration compliance advisory and renewals

A work permit is an official document allowing a foreigner to legally work in Tanzania for a specified period. Our team ensures your application is complete, submitted correctly, and tracked — to avoid costly delays or rejections.

Environmental Services (EIA)

Environmental Impact Assessment and compliance for construction, mining, agriculture, and industrial projects.

EIA Services

  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) studies and full reports
  • Submission to NEMC (National Environment Management Council)
  • Environmental & Social Impact Assessment (ESIA)
  • Environmental audit and monitoring plans
  • Project Environmental Management Plans (PEMP)
  • Environmental compliance advisory and follow-up

Who Needs an EIA in Tanzania?

Any project with potential environmental or social impact — including construction, agribusiness, mining, tourism facilities, and industrial operations — legally requires an EIA before operations begin. We guide you from the initial scoping all the way through to receiving your EIA certificate from NEMC.
